What We Do

Electrical Services Done Properly

From a single powerpoint to a full fitout — every job tested, tidied and finished to Australian Standards.

Residential Electrical

From new powerpoints and ceiling fans to full home rewires across Alfredton, Wendouree and beyond. Every job is finished neatly and tested to Australian Standards before we pack up.

24/7 Emergency Electrical

Lost power in the middle of the night or a switchboard that won't stop tripping? Call any time — we'll get you safe and sorted, day or night, weekends included.

Lighting Design & Installation

LED upgrades, downlights, outdoor and security lighting tailored to how you actually use your home or shop. We'll help you cut power bills while making the space feel right.

Safety Switches & Switchboards

Older Ballarat homes often run on switchboards that simply weren't built for modern loads. We upgrade boards and install RCDs so your family and appliances are properly protected.

Small Business & Shop Fitouts

Power, data and lighting for cafes, offices and retail spaces around Ballarat. We work around your trading hours so your customers and staff aren't disrupted.

Safety Inspections

Buying, selling, or just want peace of mind? We carry out thorough electrical safety inspections with a clear, honest report on what's safe and what needs attention.
